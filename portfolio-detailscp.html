Features of CapCut for macOS

  • Intuitive User Interface: CapCut boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it accessible for all skill levels. Its drag-and-drop functionality allows you to import media files effortlessly.
  • Trimming and Cutting: Easily trim and cut your video clips to remove unwanted portions.
  • Transitions: Choose from a variety of transitions to create smooth video transitions.
  • Filters and Effects: Enhance your videos with filters, effects, and color adjustments.
  • Text and Titles: Add text, titles, and captions to your videos with customizable fonts and animations.
  • Stickers and Elements: Spice up your videos with stickers, emojis, and graphics.
  • Audio Editing: Adjust the audio, add background music, or include voiceovers
  • 3x Speed Control
  • CapCut allows you to control the speed of your video clips, enabling you to create slow-motion or fast-motion effects.
  • Multi-Layer Editing: You can work with multiple video and audio layers, enabling more complex editing and overlaying
  • Export Options: CapCut supports various export resolutions and formats, ensuring compatibility with your target platform, be it social media, YouTube, or your website.
